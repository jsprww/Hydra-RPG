In Hydra RPG you can fight monsters, collect over 40 items, barter with merchants, play with your monsters and learn new knowledge at shrines. Hydra RPG has a rich storyline that can vary depending on how you play. Hydra RPG has one of the best shop systems out there.

Game features:

- Bestiary, to show what monsters you have catched

- Merchants, that you can buy items from

- Shrines, where you learn new skills

- Enemies, that you can kill and gain items from

- Quests, which give you loads of rewards

- Achievements, that earn you experience

- Bosses, that challenge and give you items

- Weather, which effects your stats
